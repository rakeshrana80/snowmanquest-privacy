📋 1. Introduction

Welcome to Snowman Quest ("the App"), a word-guessing mobile game developed and published as an independent app on Google Play. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Snowman Quest is designed to work without collecting any personally identifiable information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

🔍 2. Information We Collect

✅ Short answer: We do not collect any personal information. All game data is stored locally on your device.

Data stored locally on your device (never transmitted):

  • Game progress — current level, XP, and score
  • Statistics — words solved, win/loss record, accuracy
  • Hint coin balance and streak counter
  • Daily challenge completion status
  • App settings and preferences (e.g. sound on/off)

This data lives exclusively in your device's local storage (using Android's SharedPreferences and Hive database). It is never uploaded to any server and is never accessible to us or any third party.

🚫 3. Information We Do NOT Collect

Snowman Quest does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Name, email address, phone number, or any contact information
  • Location data (GPS, IP-based, or otherwise)
  • Device identifiers (IMEI, Advertising ID, etc.)
  • Contacts, photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Browsing history or activity outside the App
  • Financial or payment information
  • Any biometric data

🔐 4. App Permissions

Snowman Quest requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — Used only to load word lists and game assets if network is available. No personal data is sent over this connection.
  • VIBRATE — Optional haptic feedback on button taps (stays on-device).

The App does not request permissions for Camera, Microphone, Location, Contacts, Storage, or any other sensitive system resource.

🛡️ 5. Data Storage & Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using Android's standard secure storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences and Hive). This data is:

  • Isolated to the App's private sandbox — other apps cannot access it
  • Automatically deleted when you uninstall the App
  • Never backed up to our servers (device cloud backup, if any, is managed by your device manufacturer)

Since we do not receive or store your data on any server, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information.

🔗 6. Third-Party Services

The current version of Snowman Quest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, social login services, or crash-reporting tools.

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google may collect aggregate, anonymized data about app installs, crashes, and usage as part of their standard platform services. This collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy, not this policy.

👶 7. Children's Privacy

Snowman Quest is a family-friendly word game suitable for all ages, including children. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). Because we collect no personal information from anyone, the App is safe for children to use.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information through the App, please contact us immediately at the address below and we will take prompt action.

💰 8. In-App Purchases & Advertising

In-App Purchases: Snowman Quest does not currently offer any in-app purchases. All game features are free.

Advertising: The App may display a placeholder "Watch Ad for Hints" button as a future feature. At this time, no live advertisements are shown and no advertising SDK is integrated. If ads are added in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly and users will be notified via an app update.

🗑️ 9. Data Retention & Deletion

All data stored by Snowman Quest exists only on your personal device. You can delete all App data at any time through your device settings:

  1. Open your device Settings
  2. Go to Apps (or Application Manager)
  3. Find Snowman Quest
  4. Tap Storage → Clear Data

Alternatively, uninstalling the App will permanently remove all locally stored game data.

⚖️ 10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights under applicable privacy laws (such as GDPR for EU residents, CCPA for California residents, etc.):

  • Right to Access — You can view all your game data within the App itself
  • Right to Deletion — Clear App data via device settings at any time
  • Right to Portability — Not applicable (data is stored on your own device)
  • Right to Object — No profiling or automated decisions are made

Since we do not collect or hold any personal data on our systems, most data rights requests are satisfied automatically — your data is already under your full control.
